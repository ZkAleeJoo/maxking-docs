# PLUGIN INFORMATION

> MaxEvolutionTools is a plugin designed to reward player effort by allowing their tools to evolve and gain new abilities as they progress by mining blocks.

## 1. Tool Progression and Evolution

> The core of the plugin is designed to manage the progression of each tool individually and automatically:

* `Automatic Tracking:` Continuously and accurately tracks the number of blocks broken with specifically configured tools.
* `Milestone System:` Allows setting configurable goals that, when reached, grant automatic rewards such as new enchantments or upgrades to existing ones.
* `Dynamic Display:` Shows the tool’s current progress directly in the item name or lore using fully customizable formats.

## 2. Unlockable Special Abilities

> An advanced reward system that enhances and modifies tool behavior as higher evolution levels are reached:

* `Self-Repair:` Passively repairs the tool’s durability based on a probability percentage when mining.
* `Auto-Smelt:` Automatically smelts collected ores, allowing players to directly obtain ingots (such as iron, gold, or copper) when breaking raw ore blocks.
* `Telepathy:` Instantly transfers mined blocks and drops directly into the player’s inventory, preventing them from falling on the ground and being lost.
* `Drill:` Exclusive ability for high-tier pickaxes (Diamond and Netherite) that allows mining a full 3x3 area in a single hit.
* `Hammer:` Exclusive ability for high-tier shovels (Diamond and Netherite) designed to excavate a 3x3 area simultaneously.
* `XP-Boost:` Multiplies the amount of natural experience dropped by blocks or ores when mined.
* `Haste:` Applies the Haste potion effect continuously and passively as long as the player holds the tool in their main hand.

## 3. Counting Control and Anti-Farm System

> Detailed and intelligent configurations to ensure fair progression and prevent system abuse:

* `Material Filters:` Uses whitelist and blacklist systems to precisely define which blocks grant evolution points and which are ignored.
* `Category Validation:` Strictly verifies that the mined block matches the correct tool type (e.g., requiring an axe to collect wood), preventing incorrect farming.
* `Abuse Prevention:` By default, excludes easily farmable blocks such as leaves, crops, flowers, and vines, ensuring that progression requires genuine player effort.
