# Menus and User Interface (GUI)

MaxTools stands out for its highly polished graphical interface, with custom sounds for each interaction and an immersive design.

### Evolution Hub
The main menu (`/met hub`). It shows the current tool you have in your hand, indicating in a summarized way:
* Your current level and the next level.
* Tool usage and goals.
* Milestone and unlocked abilities progress.

### Milestone Tree
An interactive multi-page menu that maps your entire journey. Items change color according to your progress:
* 🟢 **Green Glass:** Milestones already unlocked.
* 🟡 **Yellow/Orange Glass:** Your current goal and its progress.
* 🔴 **Red Glass:** Blocked future milestones.

### Statistics and Abilities
* **Tool Statistics:** A detailed panel (`Comparator`) that shows the exact completion percentage and a summary of your abilities' activations.
* **Abilities Menu:** An exclusive panel (`Nether Star`) to review the real-time status of each ability (Active, On Cooldown, or Blocked).

### Language Selector
A dedicated panel to change the global language of the plugin instantly, visually switching between English (`config_en`) and Spanish (`config_es`) profiles.
