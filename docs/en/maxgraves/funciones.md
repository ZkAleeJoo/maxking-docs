# INFORMATION

> MaxGraves is a plugin that creates graves on your Minecraft server, allowing players to recover their items after death.

## 1. Features

* `Grave System:` Upon death, a grave is generated where all your items and experience are stored.

* `Quick Recovery:` With a single click, you can recover all your belongings. Armor and off-hand items are automatically equipped.

* `Grave Menu:` After dying, you can run the command `/mg info` to view a menu with all your active graves.

* `Teleportation Map:` Upon death, you will receive a map that teleports you directly to your grave when right-clicked. This system is controlled by the `maxgraves.tp` permission.

* `Grave Accumulation:` Graves accumulate based on the number of player deaths. The duration of each grave can be configured in the `config.yml` file.
